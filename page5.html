Day 1 Transfer to Akagera National Park
Early morning after warm breakfast you will driver straight to Akagera National Park Reception
for briefing and begin a full game drive where you will enjoy viewing wildlife such as big five
animals, antelopes, and various bird species, as well as different savannah landscapes such as
rolling hills, woodland, and open savannah grassland. You will travel to the park's northern exit
while observing various bird species. Later, return to Kigali for overnight at your hotel
Day 2 from Kigali to Nyungwe National Park
Early morning takes off from Kigali, transfer to the South Province of Rwanda in the Nyungwe
National Park. Later, you'll arrive at the Uwinka Overlook and meet the park's guide, who will
tell you about the Igishigishigi paths that lead to the Canopy Walkway, a 70-meter-high bridge.
People walk on it, taking in the breathtaking scenery and various bird species in the area, as well
as primates jumping in the trees. After that you will visit tea plantation and experiencing cultural
activities around the park overnight at your hotel in Kigali
Day 3 Nyungwe National Park kamiranzovu water fall
Early in the morning you will be back to reception and meet a guide who will give more briefing
on kamiranzovu water fall after return back to kigali at your hotel
Day 4 Mount Bisoke hike and Transfer to Kigali
Early in the morning after breakfast you will start mountain Bisoke hiking where you will see the
most attractive creator lake on the top of mountain created due to volcanic eruption later after
you will drive back to Kigali for overnight
Day 5 Kigali city tour
in morning around 9:30am you do Kigali city tour by visiting Kigali memorial sites and
parliament museum where you learn more about how Rwandan genocide against Tutsi was
planned and how it was stopped by Rwanda Defense Force and the strategies used to build
solidarity of Rwandese up to now based to the time you will visit Kaplaki crafts center for
buying handcraft souvenirs then your driver will drive you to your hotel
Day 6 king fisher resort
In the morning, we will drive to Lake Muhazi to the famed King Fisher Resort, where you will
be able to enjoy water tourist activities such as boat riding, swimming, and tasting meals cooked
by skilled chefs after returning to Kigali to your hotel.
7 Days hose riding and zipline experience
This morning at 8 a.m., you'll drive to Mountain Kigali, where you'll stop at popular locations
called Fazendas and participate in ziplining, hose riding, and arrow shooting. After lunch, you'll
travel to Umusambi Villages, where you'll see more bird species like Crested Crene and various
vegetation trees in this area, before returning to your hotel.
