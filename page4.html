Day 1: Transfer to Akagera for Full Game drive
In morning the driving guide will pick you up from Kigali International Airport and
bring you to Akagera National Park for a full day of wildlife viewing. Big five animals are
among the species that may be found here. Giraffes, several antelopes, and birds are some of the
animals. You will then return to Kigali and spend the night at your hotel.
Day 2: Visiting Fazenda Sengha
In morning a driver guide will pick you up from your hotel and transport you to the
fazenda, where you will participate in various activities such as quad biking, ziplining, and archery.
Depending on time, you will then visit the Kigali Genocide Memorial before returning to your hotel.
Day 3: Canopy Walk away in Nyungwe national park
In morning will drive into Nyungwe National Park to the Uwinka reception
where our site guide will give us a briefing on the canopy walkway, which is a 70-meter-high
bridge while we watch the spectacular view and different bird species around us, as well as
primates jumping in the trees, after which you will visit the Gisakura Tea Plantation. And then
back to Kigali to your hotel or catch-up fright

Accommodation ranges 4 to 5star hotel
