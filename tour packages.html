Day 1 arrival in Kigali international airport and transfer to your hotel
Checking at phoenix plaza Apartment with worm welcome with a professional staff, based to the time you will do night walk in Kigali city and enjoying the special view of the city and back to the phoenix plaza for overnight.
Day 2: Full game drive
Early morning starting the journey from Kigali to Akagera National Park reception for briefing and start full game drive where you will enjoy viewing wildlife like big five animals, antelopes and different bird species, different savannah landscape: a spectacular view of rolling hills, woodland, and open savannah grassland, after that you will check-in at Akagera Rhono Lodge where you will sleep in a very quiet place.
Day 3: Bird watching and back to Kigali
Early in the morning wake up for breakfast pack your staff and check out with the lodge and your driver and the guide will be waiting for you out there. You will drive to the northern exit of the park while watching different bird species like black-headed ganolex, the lilac-breasted roller, and many more others in this park that host about 480 bird species. Late then drive back to Kigali for overnight at phoenix plaza Apartment
Day 4: Canopy Walk
Early morning drive from Kigali to Nyungwe National Park at Uwinka reception where we will meet with site guide and give us a briefing for Canopy walkway which is 70M Height Bridge while watching the spectacular view and different bird species around and also primates jumping in trees, most of the monkeys you will see rare L’Hoest’s monkey, vervet monkeys. Overnight at Nyungwe Top View Hill Hotel
Day 5: Kamiranzovu Waterfall
Early morning after breakfast and check out, you will head to Gisakura reception and meet with site guide who will guide you to Kamiranzovu Waterfall which is quite an attractive site in the mildest of the park once you reach there you feel like you are in paradise after you will head back to phoenix plaza Apartment
Day 6: Golden Monkey Trekking
Early morning after breakfast leaves the hotel then drives to RDB offices for Golden Monkeys trekking briefing. Our driver takes you to the foot of the volcanoes to meet your monkey group by walking, the visit last between 1h30 and 3hours, after golden monkey’s visit, drive back to Gorilla Hotel
Day 7: Gorilla guardian villages
After breakfast, you will be driven to Gorilla guardian Village to learn more about Rwandan traditional life styles such as banana beer manufacture, traditional dance, arrow shooter, traditional healing, and other entertainment. Kivu serena hotel
Days 8: Boat ride in Lake Kivu
In The morning after breakfast and with a view of Lake Kivu, you will have long boat ride into the lake, from there you shall visit one of the biggest islands in that lake, like Napoleon Island, when we find some bird species, Peace Island, from there you will be back at your hotel for lunch and check out in the hotel and drive back to Kigali for overnight. Phoenix plaza
Day 9: Nature walks at Nyandungu Urban Wetland Eco-tourism Park and Umusambi village
After breakfast, at approximately 8:00 a.m., you will drive to Nyandungu Urban Wetland Ecotourism Park, which is the best place to relax in Kigali by watching different bird species and other vegetation conserved in this park. It is also a good place for taking pictures with a beautiful view.  After that, you'll go to lunch. After lunch, you will go to Umusambi Village, which is near Nyandungu Urban Wetland Eco-tourism Park. Umusambi Village is a beautifully restored wetland
area that houses over 50 endangered Grey Crowned Cranes rescued from the illegal pet trade. When you visit Umusambi Village, you can see these magnificent birds enjoying their new home. Explore the wetland area and be immersed in nature by using network of walking trails. Overnight stay at the phoenix plaza Apartment.
Day 10: Kigali City Tour
In the morning after comfortable breakfast, you will start a city tour in Kigali by visiting Fazenda at mount Kigali, there you will enjoy Zipline, horse and buggy riding, and archery, later after lunch you will visit Gisozi memorial site for learning more about genocide against Tutsi of 1994, and campaign against genocide. Depending on available time from there you can visit Kimironko local market and drive back to the phoenix plaza Apartment.
Day 11: End of the trip
Around 9:00 a.m., you will drive to downtown Rwanda to visit various shops and malls where you will be free to buy anything related to Rwandan culture as travel souvenirs. You will travel from downtown to Caplaki Craft Village, the best and largest handcrafts shop. This Caplaki Craft Village has more treasures representing Rwanda and East African parties. Return to the hotel for check-out and transfer to Kigali International Airport.
