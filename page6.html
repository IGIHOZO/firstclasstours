Day 1: Full game drive (akagera NP)
Early morning starting the journey from Kigali to Akagera National Park reception for briefing
and start full game drive where you will enjoy viewing wildlife like big five animals, antelopes
and different bird species, different savannah landscape: a spectacular view of rolling hills,
woodland, and open savannah grassland, Late then drive back to Kigali for overnight
Day 1: Gorilla Trekking (Volcanoes NP)
After breakfast, drive to Kinigi for gorilla trekking. Before 7 a.m., you will be briefed on the visit
to the RDB tourism office. Following the briefing, you will be assigned to a gorilla group to visit,
and you will then return to your car and proceed to the park gate to begin your gorilla trekking.
Late in the evening, return to Kigali for the overnight.
