Day 1 arrival in Kigali international airport and transfer to your hotel Checking at Hotel with worm welcome with a professional staff, based to the time you will do night walk in Kigali city and enjoying the special view of the city and back to the hotel for overnight.
Day 2: Full game drive
Early morning starting the journey from Kigali to Akagera National Park reception for briefing and start full game drive where you will enjoy viewing wildlife like big five animals, antelopes and different bird species, different savannah landscape: a spectacular view of rolling hills, woodland, and open savannah grassland, after that you will check-in at your hotel where you will sleep in a very quiet place.
Day 3: boat riding and fishing
Early in the morning wake up for breakfast pack your staff and check out with the lodge and your driver and the guide will be waiting for you out there. You will drive to the lake Shakani for boat riding and fishing then drive to northern exit of the park while watching different bird species and other wild animals, late then drive back to Kigali for overnight at your hotel
Day 4: Canopy Walk
Early morning drive from Kigali to Nyungwe National Park at Uwinka reception where we will meet with site guide and give us a briefing for Canopy walkway which is 70M Height Bridge while watching the spectacular view and different bird species around and also primates jumping in trees, most of the monkeys you will see rare L’Hoest’s monkey, vervet monkeys. Overnight at your Hotel
Day 5: Kamiranzovu Waterfall
Early morning after breakfast and check out, you will head to Gisakura reception and meet with site guide who will guide you to Kamiranzovu Waterfall which is quite an attractive site in the mildest of the park once you reach there you feel like you are in paradise after you will head to your Hotel
Day 6: Golde Monkey Trekking
Early morning after breakfast leaves the hotel then drives to RDB offices for Golden Monkeys trekking briefing. Our driver takes you to the foot of the volcanoes to meet your monkey group by walking, the visit last between 1h30 and 3hours, after golden monkey’s visit, your Hotel
Day 7: mountain Bisoke Climbing
After breakfast, you will be driven to RDB office for briefing on does and don’t during hiking after that you will drive where you will leave the car and start our hiking with professional guide at the top of mountain you will see the nice view of crater lake and it will be a good time to enjoy your packet lunch then return back where we packed our car. Drive to your hotel
Days 8: Boat ride in Lake Kivu
In The morning after breakfast and with a view of Lake Kivu, you will drive to other side of lake Kivu from there you shall visit one of the biggest islands in that lake, like Napoleon Island, when
we find some bird species, Peace Island, from there you will be back at your hotel for lunch and check out in the hotel and drive back to Kigali for overnight. your Hotel
Days 9: Relaxing to Kivu Serena Hotel Beach
In morning after worm breakfast around 9am you will have a chance to enjoy nice view at beach it will be a good chance for doing activities in water like swimming in hotel swimming pool and boat riding, after these activities we shall take lunch at hotel then drive back to Kigali for overnight at your hotel
Day 10: Nature walks at Nyandungu Urban Wetland Eco-tourism Park and Umusambi village
After breakfast, at approximately 8:00 a.m., you will drive to Nyandungu Urban Wetland Eco-tourism Park, which is the best place to relax in Kigali by watching different bird species and other vegetation conserved in this park. It is also a good place for taking pictures with a beautiful view. After that, you'll go to lunch. After lunch, you will go to Umusambi Village, which is near Nyandungu Urban Wetland Eco-tourism Park. Umusambi Village is a beautifully restored wetland area that houses over 50 endangered Grey Crowned Cranes rescued from the illegal pet trade. When you visit Umusambi Village, you can see these magnificent birds enjoying their new home. Explore the wetland area and be immersed in nature by using network of walking trails. Overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 11: Kigali City Tour
In the morning after comfortable breakfast, you will start a city tour in Kigali by visiting Fazenda at mount Kigali, there you will enjoy Zipline, buggy riding, and archery, later after lunch you will visit Gisozi memorial site for learning more about genocide against Tutsi of 1994, and campaign against genocide. Depending on available time from there you can visit Kimironko local market and drive back to the hotel.
12 Shopping and transfer to Kigali international airport
Around 9:00 a.m., you will drive to downtown Rwanda to visit various shops and malls where you will be free to buy anything related to Rwandan culture as travel souvenirs. You will travel from downtown to Caplaki Craft Village, the best and largest handcrafts shop. This Caplaki Craft Village has more treasures representing Rwanda and East African parties. Return to the hotel for check-out and transfer to Kigali International Airport.
